Welcome to Skits & Scripts

Welcome to a unique and engaging group where people of many interests can come together to share skits and create memorable experiences. Our group is for people of all experience levels, with beginners encouraged. This is not an acting class, but a fun and interesting way for people to get together. We will explore various topics from serious to silly in a relaxed way as we expand ourselves and our social connections.

We are based in the Elmwood district of Berkeley on College Ave., only a block from the Elmwood Theater.

Whether you’re here to try your hand at scriptwriting, expand your awareness or just have fun acting out skits, this in-person group is the perfect way to express yourself, learn about others and add a little more joy to your life. Participation is key, so we hope that everyone will be performing, writing and making new friends.

We started out by using Meetup, but we want to be open to anyone, regardless of Meetup.

What We’re About

Role-Play for Everyone: No experience? That’s fine. Enjoy acting in a laid-back atmosphere with no costumes or props required. No memorization required, but reading proficiency is crucial.

Creative Writing Made Easier: Discover how simple and efficient it can be to create various types of scripts, using effective prompts and editing with tools like Chat GPT.

Fun and Connection with Others: Bring more humor and people into your life while finding new possibilities to break the ice at social gatherings.

Personal Growth: Explore self-discovery and gain new perspectives by playing different characters and diving into meaningful topics. Skits offer unique freedom to look at serious issues creatively.

A Community of Creators: Meet people from diverse backgrounds who share your enthusiasm for sharing and having new experiences.
