About

Planet A is unlike any production you have ever seen or heard of.

The cast consists of live human actors, as well as aliens.

The content consists of direct moral challenges to humanity.

The production includes immersive multimedia and a computer-controlled lighting system.

Planet A is definitely scary, mostly because human beings are.

Planet A is also extraordinarily funny, charming and engaging.

Plus, free food!
